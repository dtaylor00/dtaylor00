What is this?
This is a project to convert a Latin-script sentence (abcd...) to a Kishimu-script sentence. The Kishimu-script is an alternative writing system for a fantasy species known as Kishimus and was created by Paradoxum. Check out more of her work and art here: https://www.instagram.com/paradoxum_art/. The Kishimu script uses an alphasyllabary (or abugida) system whereas the Latin script uses an alphabet system. Alphasyllabary is similar to an alphabet, but its vowels are not full standalone symbols. Instead, the vowels must be appended or attached to a consonant. The Kishimu script has two more main differences compared to Latin script. The first, words in a sentence are written without break (similar to cursive).
